Commands
Use the number pad or the arrow keys to move. Hold down multiple arrow keys to move diagonally.
WASD and vi keys work too.

Attack by pressing [Z] and then a direction.
Similarly, lunge by pressing [X] and then a direction.

Attacks and lunges are executed the turn after you input them, so plan your moves carefully. Enemies will have a chance to move out of the way of an attack.

Open this help screen again with the [?] key.

Some sprites are based off of Lovable Rogue and eight2empire.
